1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how the PDF Merger – Split & Convert app ("the App") handles your information. By using the App you agree to the terms described below. The App is designed to process your files entirely on your device — nothing is ever uploaded to a server.

Personal data

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email address, or the contents of your documents.

Limited technical information — such as device type, operating system version, and anonymised crash logs — may be collected automatically to help us improve stability and performance.

The App displays ads provided by Huawei Petal Ads. Petal Ads may collect anonymous identifiers (such as the Huawei advertising ID) to serve relevant ads. This data is governed by Huawei's Privacy Policy. You can reset or opt out of personalised ads in your device's Huawei ID settings.

3. How we use information
The limited information described above is used solely to:

Improve app performance and reliability.
Display ads that keep the App free for users.
We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party beyond the ad partner described in Section 2.

4. Local processing — your files stay on your device
All PDF operations — merging, splitting, converting, and extracting pages — are performed entirely on your device. Your PDF files and images are never transmitted to any external server. Processed files are saved to your local device storage only, and only with your explicit action.

6. Data security
Because all file processing is local and we do not store or transmit your documents, the risk of unauthorised access to your files through our service is minimal. We recommend keeping your device up to date with the latest security patches.

7. Children's privacy
The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
